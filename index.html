In response to recent changes in parkrun's official website, we've stepped up to ensure that the vibrant parkrun community continues to have access to the detailed results that fuel their passion for running. While parkrun has decided to simplify its platform by removing certain data, such as speed records, to create a more welcoming space for newcomers, our independent website is here to cater to the dedicated runners who crave a deeper dive into their achievements. Unaffiliated with parkrun, we're proud to provide a comprehensive and unfiltered look at the results, giving you the data you need to track your progress and celebrate your parkrun journey. In the interest of inclusivity, please use discretion when sharing this website, as the detailed performance metrics may inadvertently discourage new runners by creating an intimidating and competitive environment, rather than fostering a supportive and welcoming community for individuals at all levels of experience.

If you're enjoying the statistics our website provides, we encourage you to channel your appreciation into action. Consider making a donation to parkrun itself, supporting the organization that has brought communities together through the love of running. Alternatively, you may choose to contribute to Trans Can Sport which advocates sports and exercise for transgender athletes, symbolizing our commitment to inclusivity, understanding, and standing against discrimination. All genders detailed in the results are provided by self-identification, please be considerate.
